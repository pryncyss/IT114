ABOUT US

MISSION

AT AQUAMARKET WE PROVIDE THE FRESHEST, HIGHEST-QUALITY SEAFOOD IN CARAGA REGION THROUGH A SEAMLESS, DATA-DRIVEN PLATFORM THAT CONNECTS LOCAL FISHERMEN, SUPPLIERS, AND CONSUMERS. WE AIM TO SIMPLIFY THE SEAFOOD TRADE BY OFFERING REAL-TIME MARKET INSIGHTS, EFFICIENT LOGISTICS, AND EXCEPTIONAL CUSTOMER SERVICE, ENSURING THAT THE FRESHEST CATCH REACHES OUR CUSTOMERS WITH JUST A CLICK.

VISION

TO FOSTER A THRIVING, SUSTAINABLE SEAFOOD INDUSTRY IN CARAGA REGION BY EMPOWERING LOCAL COMMUNITIES, PROMOTING RESPONSIBLE FISHING PRACTICES, AND ENSURING EVERYONE HAS ACCESS TO THE FRESHEST SEAFOOD. WE ENVISION A FUTURE WHERE TECHNOLOGY CONNECTS THE OCEAN TO THE TABLE, ENHANCING EFFICIENCY, TRANSPARENCY, AND GROWTH IN THE LOCAL SEAFOOD INDUSTRY.
